How to use this app:

  • This app must be used on a phone/tablet
  • Press PLAY to start your synth
  • The volume decreases when you move your phone
  • But you can turn off the inverse with the inverse button
  • Control a filter by moving your phone the Y axis
  • Control pitch by moving your phone on the X axis
