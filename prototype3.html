How to use this app:
(NB: be prepared that it might take some time to load the sounds):

  • This app must be used on a phone/tablet
  • Tap the button to start (tap it again to switch between different loops)
  • Tilt your phone to control the loops
  • The volume decreases when you move your phone. Use the Inverse button to get an opposite effect.
